# GENERAL WORKPLACE POLICIES & EMERGENCY GUIDELINES

Document Ref: OFFICE-POL-101
Clearance Requirement: Public / Unclassified.
Last Updated: March 10, 2026

## 1. CORE WORKING HOURS AND REMOTE WORK
*   **Core Hours:** All employees are expected to be available online or in-office during our core hours of 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time. Flexible arrival and departure times are allowed outside of these core hours.
*   **Remote Work Policy:** Employees can work remotely up to two (2) days per week with prior written manager approval.

## 2. CAFETERIA SERVICES & MEAL TIMES
*   **Daily Cafeteria Schedule:** The ground floor cafeteria serves breakfast from 08:00 AM to 09:30 AM, and lunch from 12:00 PM to 02:00 PM.
*   **Special Menu Wednesdays:** Every Wednesday features the traditional regional menu and complimentary herbal tea options.

## 3. IN CASE OF EMERGENCY
*   For physical facility emergencies, contact Building Security at extension 9111.
*   The designated outdoor assembly point during fire alarms is the Central Courtyard next to the flagpole.
