# IT DEPT SUPPORT GUIDE & INTERNAL NETWORK CONFIGURATIONS

Document Ref: IT-FAQ-9092
Clearance Requirement: Level 1 (Confidential) and above.
Last Updated: May 2, 2026

## 1. SECURE VPN INTRANET ACCESS
Employees working remotely must use the secure government VPN client (GovConnect v4.2) to access internal databases.
*   **Connection URL:** The VPN endpoint is `https://vpn.intranet.secure.gov`.
*   **Authentication:** Requires two-factor authentication (2FA) via the approved hardware token or Microsoft Authenticator app.
*   **Password Policy:** Passwords must be at least 16 characters, containing uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Passwords expire every 90 days.

## 2. SETTING UP LOCAL PRINTER ACCESS
To connect to the floor printers:
1. Open the System Settings on your workstation.
2. Select "Printers & Scanners" and click "Add Printer".
3. Enter the network IP address: `10.102.50.45` for the Department Color Laserjet (Floor 2, Room 202).
4. The system will automatically fetch the driver package from the intranet repository.
5. If prompted for admin authentication, contact the IT desk at extension 4900.
